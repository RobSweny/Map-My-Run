Next step is easy

Grab your shoes, start the app and start walking/running.

The map shows areas that were previously covered by runners, so the next choice is yours. Follow a proven path or lay your won trail, explore and map your area.

Finished your trip?

If you are finished your run, then upload your results and check out your trip on the map!
We plan to include more functionality such as showing who our biggest explorers are and more personalised information. So keep mapping and stay tuned!
